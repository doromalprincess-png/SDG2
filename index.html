What is SDG 2: Zero Hunger?

SDG 2 aims to end hunger, improve food security, promote sustainable agriculture, and ensure everyone has access to safe and nutritious food. This website supports SDG 2 by promoting sustainable cooking and helping people make greener ingredient choices.
