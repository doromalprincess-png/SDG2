About This Project

Welcome to Zero Hunger! This project supports SDG 2: Zero Hunger by promoting sustainable food choices and local agriculture.

Our mission is to help users understand the environmental impact of their meals and make simple, eco-friendly ingredient swaps.

By using this platform, you contribute to a healthier planet while enjoying delicious Filipino dishes. Share your recipes, explore swaps, and join our journey toward a world without hunger!
